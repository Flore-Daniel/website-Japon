Some information

Located in eastern Asia, Japan is made up of 14,125 islands, including 4 large archipelagos. Japan, known as the "Land of the Rising Sun," is an island nation in East Asia with a rich history spanning thousands of years. Renowned for its blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology, Japan offers a unique cultural experience. From bustling cities like Tokyo and Osaka to serene temples in Kyoto, each region showcases its own charm. Japan’s landscapes range from beautiful cherry blossom gardens to majestic Mount Fuji. With a deep respect for nature, art, and innovation, Japan continues to fascinate and inspire visitors from around the world.
